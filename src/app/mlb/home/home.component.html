MLB Stats

Game Log
Game log for an MLB team. Currently hard-coded to the regular season for the 2018 Baltimore Orioles.
Runs Per Hit
Report of all 30 MLB teams, initially sorted by overall standings, that lists each team's runs, hits, and two computted columns (Runs per Hit and Hits per Run). Currently for the 2018 regular season.
Standings
Overall and league standings. Only supports the 2018 regular season at this point.
